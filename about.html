Q-Bot was a project I created in March 2021. I have always been fascinated by artificial intelligence and had dreams of creating my own. You can ask Q-Bot certain questions and it will respond to you. It is important to avoid typos in order for Q-Bot to give you an accurate response. Q-Bot is programmed entirely in JavaScript.

The Old Q-Bot...

When Q-Bot was first launched, it was only accessible on desktop. About 2 months later I learned the Bootstrap library and spent a night redesigning the site. I am very happy with the changes I have made, and I have so much fun talking to Q-bot on my mobile devices!
